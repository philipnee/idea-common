A board for sparks.

Most ideas do not begin as products. They begin as a sentence, a note in the middle of the day, or a thought you do not want to lose.

Litboard is a public place to put those sparks where other people can see them. Post the idea, add context if it helps, and let the good ones pick up some heat.

The match is there for a reason. One fire means someone noticed. More fire means the idea keeps traveling.
