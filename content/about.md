About litboard.net

A public board for ideas. Post one, see if it catches fire.
That's it. No signup to browse. No comments. No algorithms. Just ideas and a heat signal that tells you if anyone cares.

How it works

Post an idea — a title, a one-liner, a pitch if you want. Anyone can see it. Anyone can fire it.
Every idea starts cold. As people view it and fire it, the heat rises:

💨 Ash — it exists. Someone glanced at it.

🔥 Ember — a quiet glow. People are curious.

🔥🔥 Spark — attention is building.

🔥🔥🔥 Flame — the crowd is paying attention.

🔥🔥🔥🔥 Blaze — real momentum.

🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥 Wildfire — stop reading this and go build it.

Heat isn't a like count. You'll never see a number. It's based on sustained attention from real people over time. A thousand clicks from one person means nothing. Ten people showing up every day for a week means everything.

Why this exists

Most ideas die in the shower, a notes app, or a group chat where someone replies "cool" and changes the subject. Some of them deserved better.
Litboard is where they get a shot.
